# Sales-Dashboard-PowerBI
A real-world Business Intelligence project completed as part of a Data Analyst Assignment by KS AI & Cloud Solutions.
📌 Problem Statement
A regional sales company needed a proper reporting system to track sales performance across regions, managers, and product categories. Their existing process was manual and scattered across spreadsheets.

📊 Dashboard Pages

Business Overview — Sales, Profit, Orders & YoY Growth
Region & Manager Performance
Category Target Achievement
Returns Analysis
Delivery Analysis

💡 Key Insights

Furniture achieving only 74% of its sales target
Technology & Office Supplies exceeding targets
Delivery delay rate at 88.69% — major operational risk
Overall sales grew 54.97% YoY

🛠️ Tools & Skills
Power BI · Power Query · DAX · Data Modeling · Dashboard Design
